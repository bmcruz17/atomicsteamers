Know the Signs

Signs of Bed Bugs in Your Oahu Home

Caught early, bed bugs are faster and cheaper to wipe out. Watch for these.

  • Itchy bites in rows — small red bites on arms, legs, or back after sleeping.
  • Blood spots — tiny rust-colored stains on sheets and pillowcases.
  • Dark specks — fecal spots along mattress seams, box springs, and headboards.
  • Shed skins & eggs — pale husks and tiny white eggs in cracks and crevices.
  • Musty odor — a sweet, musty smell in heavily infested rooms.
  • Live bugs — flat, apple-seed-sized, reddish-brown insects in seams and folds.
