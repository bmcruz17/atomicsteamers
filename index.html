Good to Know

Bed Bug Questions, Answered

Does steam really kill bed bugs and their eggs?

Yes. Bed bugs and their eggs die almost instantly when exposed to temperatures above 120°F. Our professional steamers reach 180°+ and penetrate deep into seams, cracks, and fabric where bugs hide — killing every life stage on contact.

Is steam treatment safe for my family and pets?

Absolutely. Steam uses nothing but heat and water — no pesticides, no toxic residue. In most cases you and your pets can stay in the home, and there's no airing-out period afterward.

Will I have to throw out my mattress or furniture?

Usually not. The whole point of steam is to treat the items you already own. We steam mattresses, box springs, couches, and more directly, so you keep your belongings instead of replacing them.

How soon can you come out?

We offer same-week appointments across Oahu and can often respond faster for urgent infestations. Call us and we'll find the soonest slot for your area.

How do I know if I actually have bed bugs?

Common signs include small itchy bites in rows, tiny blood spots on sheets, dark fecal specks along mattress seams, and a faint musty odor. Not sure? Our free inspection confirms it for you.

How much does treatment cost?

Pricing depends on the size of your space and severity of the infestation. That's why we start with a free, no-obligation inspection and give you a clear, upfront quote before any work begins.
