Know the Signs

Signs of Bed Bugs in Kailua

  • Itchy bites in rows — on arms, legs, or back after sleeping.
  • Blood spots — rust-colored stains on sheets.
  • Dark specks — fecal spots along mattress seams.
  • Shed skins & eggs — in cracks and crevices.
  • Musty odor — in heavily infested rooms.
  • Live bugs — flat, apple-seed-sized, reddish-brown.
